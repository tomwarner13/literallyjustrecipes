## Ingredients
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1/3 cup chopped pine nuts or walnuts[^1]
- 2/3 cup grated Parmesan or Romano cheese
- 1/4 cup fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves, stems removed
- 4 oz fresh basil leaves, stems removed
- 1 cup EVOO, plus more if desired
- juice of 1 lemon
- salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Add the garlic, cheese, and nuts to a food processor. Pulse until they are the size of small pebbles.
2. Add the basil and parsley and pulse until the leaves are shredded into a uniform paste.
3. Transfer to a bowl. Stir in the olive oil, salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Adjust salt and pepper to taste. Store sealed and refrigerated.

Makes roughly 3 cups.

[^1]: Pine nuts are the "authentic" choice. They are also significantly more expensive than walnuts. Many people prefer the taste of walnuts anyway.
