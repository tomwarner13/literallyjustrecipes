## Ingredients
- 1 large engl\*sh cucumber
- 1 small white onion
- 8 oz (1 brick) cream cheese, room temp
- 1/4 cup unflavored Greek yogurt
- 1 tsp green hot sauce
- pinch kosher salt
- 1 tsp finely minced fresh dill

## Instructions

1. Peel and halve the cucumber. Use a spoon to scoop out the seeds (it's ok if you miss a few). Peel the onion.
2. Grate the cucumber and onion into a fine mesh strainer over a bowl. Press down to force out excess juice. Sprinkle with salt and let sit for at least 1 hour. Discard the drained juices.
3. In a large bowl, combine drained veggies with all other ingredients. Mix until smooth with a wooden spoon or an electric mixer. Taste and adjust for salt/spice levels if necessary.
4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
5. Serve with sliced veggies or crackers, or spread onto sandwiches.

Makes roughly 3 cups of dip.
