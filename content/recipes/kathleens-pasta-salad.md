## Ingredients
- 1 lb bowtie pasta
- 2 cups [pesto](/literallyjustrecipes/recipes/pesto/)
- 1 green bell pepper
- 1 red bell pepper
- 12 oz (2 small cans) black olives, pitted and sliced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes
- 4 oz cheddar cheese, optional

## Instructions

1. Cook the pasta according to package directions. Drain it, rinse it, and leave it to cool.
2. Remove seeds and stems from the peppers, and dice them well. Halve or quarter the cherry tomatoes. Drain the canned olives.
3. If using the cheese, cut it into small cubes.
4. Add pasta, pesto, diced pepper, olive, tomatoes, and cheese if using to a large bowl and mix well together.
5. Cover and refrigerate; serve cold.

Makes roughly 12 servings.
